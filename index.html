ABOUT ME

Chethan Karur is a photographer based in Bangalore with over 4 years of experience. It was his Bachelors degree in Bangalore when he started exploring the extremes, experimenting with zeros and reaching out for infinity. Chethan started off free lancing, bagging a few awards on his way, before taking a dive to establish his own firm ‘Chethan Karur’s Photography’ here in Bangalore, Karnataka.

His work spans Baby Photography, Wildlife & Nature Photography ,Corporate shoots, Product Walkthroughs and Weddings. His photographs were also featured in renowned magazines like ‘Asian Photography’ , ‘Better Photography’ etc.
Despite his awards and achievements in versatile genres,he has his best interests in Baby Photography . His portfolio has earned him recognition in major cities like Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad as one of the most approached Baby photographers.
